Flash your noisebox

Web Serial firmware installer for M5Cardputer-Adv

Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera. Safari and Firefox don't work.

1. Plug in

Connect your Cardputer-Adv to this computer with a USB-C cable. It should appear as a serial device.

2. Connect & flash

3. After flashing

  1. The device boots into a splash screen — press enter.
  2. Pick your Wi-Fi network and enter the password.
  3. Relay setup: type your bot token (from @BotFather) and your relay domain. Both are saved on the device — you won't be asked again.
  4. The device shows a 6-digit code. In Telegram DM your bot: /pair NNNNNN.
